Are you struggling to make sense of all the AI hype? You are not alone.

Written by : Olanrewaju Ezekiel

May 10 2023

Photo from unsplash

Sometimes it can seem like people are hyping up these innovations to be more than they really are.
Even if AI was designed with the intention of creating another human, it would not be capable of doing so. AI is simply a tool created by humans for human use, and it has limitations just like any other technology.

While it may have practical applications in various fields, it cannot replicate the complex emotions, thoughts, and experiences that make us uniquely human.

However, as someone who has seen firsthand the transformative impact of technology, I also believe it's important to recognize the incredible potential that AI holds.
When I think about how far we've come in just the last century, it's truly mind-boggling. One hundred years ago, flying in the air was nothing more than a fanciful dream. Today, we can travel anywhere in the world in a matter of hours.

Communication across borders used to be slow, expensive, and unreliable - now we can connect with people from all over the globe with just a few clicks.

And let's not forget the internet, which has completely revolutionized the way we access information and connect with each other. Before the internet, we had to rely on traditional media outlets to learn about what was happening in the world, and there was often no way to fact-check or verify the information we received. Now, we have a wealth of information at our fingertips, and we can use it to educate ourselves and make informed decisions.

AI is just the latest in a long line of technological advancements, but what sets it apart is its ability to automate tasks that were once thought to require human intelligence. This has the potential to be a game-changer in fields ranging from healthcare to finance to transportation.

Of course, like any tool, AI has its limitations and drawbacks, But by embracing the potential of AI and using it responsibly, we can work towards solving some of the biggest problems facing our world today.

So don't get discouraged by all the hype - instead, try to stay informed and curious about the possibilities that AI holds. Who knows what other amazing innovations we'll see in our lifetime? I, for one, can't wait to find out.
