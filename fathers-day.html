Listen up! If your father is still alive

Written by : Olanrewaju Ezekiel

April 4 2023

Photo from unsplash

If you're still blessed with the presence of your father, listen up!

Have you ever wondered who you love more - your mother or father? Many of us have a soft spot for our mother, and it's not entirely our fault. Growing up, many of us experienced a dynamic where our mothers were the primary caretakers while our fathers were busy working to provide for the family. While they worked tirelessly to ensure that we never lacked anything, their absence in our lives left a void that we didn't quite understand.

As children, we couldn't comprehend why our fathers couldn't be present at important events or why they missed out on significant moments in our lives. It was easy for us to assume that our mothers loved us more because they were always there for us when we needed them. But the truth is, our fathers' sacrifices often go unnoticed. They work hard to ensure that our basic needs are met and that we have the resources to pursue our dreams. They may not be as present as our mothers, but their love is just as strong.

Many fathers sacrifice their own dreams so that their children can have a better life. They work long hours, sometimes even giving up their own health, to provide for their families. They serve as modern-day slaves, working tirelessly to ensure that their children can dine like royalty.

If your father is still privileged to be alive, It's time to do something special for the man who gave you life and made countless sacrifices to provide for you.

I LOVE YOU DADDY :

First of all, pick up the phone and call or text your father. Tell him how much you love him and thank him for everything he's done for you. You may not have understood his sacrifices when you were younger, but now is the time to show him how much you appreciate him.

HUG HIM:

It's a sad reality that many children have never had the chance to hug their fathers, especially sons. But it's never too late to change that. If you live away from your father, make plans to visit him as soon as possible. And when you do, don't hesitate to give him a warm and heartfelt hug. Let him know how much he means to you and how grateful you are for all that he has done for you.

PRAY FOR ME:

Finally, ask your father to pray for you. Parental blessings are powerful, and they can go a long way in shaping your future. Kneel before your father and let him know that you want his blessings and guidance as you navigate through life.

CONCLUSION:
In conclusion, take the time to acknowledge your father's sacrifices while he's still with you. Don't wait until it's too late to show him how much he means to you. Remember, tomorrow may never come. So, make the most of the time you have with your father and let him know how much you love him.

HAPPY FATHERS DAY !!
