Yes, I am a murderer!

Written by : Olanrewaju Ezekiel

May 15 2023

Photo from unsplash

Millions left to perish, one man has fallen. How dare you accuse me of being a murderer!

When my privacy was violated, your voice remained silent. Even as they invaded my food, and placed poison for me to consume, you remained mute.

They conspired against me, subjecting me to countless abuses, fully aware of my helplessness.

No longer will I endure such torment. I have reached my breaking point, incapable of enduring further abuse.

The battle lines have been drawn, and I am prepared to face the consequences that may follow.

I will seek revenge relentlessly, even if it means sacrificing my last breath before surrendering in this fight.

Call me what you will, for I shall continue to eradicate these WALL GECKOS until they cease to exist.

Selah!
